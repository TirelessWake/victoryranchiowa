Our Programs

  • Kiddie Up

    Boys and Girls Ages 2-8

    Fun along with snacks and lunch for our littlest ranchers.

  • Hearts & Horses

    Boys and Girls Grades K - 12

    For those dealing with grief/loss or other difficult circumstances who need some extra attention. Participants will be offered one on one sessions with a leader and a horse in addition to group and family sessions.

  • Cowgirls

    Girls Ages 6-12

    Young girls will be challenged to overcome fear and build skills and confidence to help them make good decisions day by day.

  • Cowboys

    Boys Ages 6 - 16

    Boys will be challenged to overcome fear and build skills and confidence to help them make good decisions day by day.

  • Cowgirls Teen

    Girls Ages 13 - 16

    Teenage girls will be challenged to overcome fear and build skills and confidence to help them make good decisions day by day.

  • Horse Sense for Youth

    High School Students

    We will use the servant leadership model “Lead Like Jesus” with integrated horse activities to help students assess their own leadership skills. The horses will show them effective ways of positively influencing the behavior of others. Topics of focus include trust, respect, teamwork and communication.

  • Seed to Seat

    All Families

    Families will come together to create healthy meals at the ranch.

  • Grandparents Raising Grandchildren

    All Ages

    Informational sessions for grandparents provided while their grandchildren are engaged in fun ranch activities.
